About us

Yabo Microfinance Bank is a trusted financial partner dedicated to empowering individuals, small businesses, and communities. Established to provide inclusive banking services, we offer innovative financial solutions tailored to meet the needs of low-income earners, entrepreneurs, and underbanked populations across our region.

With a deep commitment to financial inclusion and economic growth, Yabo MFB stands as a pillar of support for those striving to build better livelihoods.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, reliable, and affordable financial services that promote economic empowerment, improve livelihoods, and foster sustainable development within our communities.

Our Vision

To become a leading microfinance institution known for transforming lives, supporting micro and small enterprises, and driving inclusive financial growth in Nigeria.
